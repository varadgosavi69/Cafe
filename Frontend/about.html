Our Story

From a single-origin obsession in a tiny Milan roastery
to a space where coffee becomes ceremony.

01
The Beginning

A single bean.
A thousand questions.

It started in 2019 when our founder, Elena Morano, couldn't find a cup of coffee in Milan that matched the ones she'd tasted in Addis Ababa. So she flew back, learned from a farmer named Tesfa, and carried an 8kg bag of green beans home.

Six months later, in a 14-square-metre roastery in Navigli, Karma Café was born — a name coined one foggy morning when the aroma of a fresh roast seemed to make everything lighter.

🫘
🔥
02
The Craft

Roasting is
listening to fire.

We roast in small batches — never more than 5kg at a time. We believe that mass production and great coffee are mutually exclusive. Every roast is logged, tasted, and refined.

Our head roaster, Marco, has over 12 years of experience and a peculiar gift: he can predict the first crack within 4 seconds just by sound. "The beans speak," he says. "Most people just don't know how to listen."

03
The Space

A room where
time floats.

Our café is designed to slow you down. No screens, no playlists that demand your attention, no rush. The warm espresso-brown walls, low lighting, and the quiet hiss of steam are intentional.

We partnered with architect Livia Russo to create a space that feels like the inside of a cup — dark at the edges, luminous at the centre. Every surface has been chosen to age beautifully.

🏮
What we stand for

Four principles.
Zero compromise.

🌱

Traceability

Every bean has a story. We know the farm, the farmer, the altitude, and the harvest year.

⚡

Precision

Temperature to 0.1°C. Grind to 0.1g. Extraction to within 2 seconds. Always.

🤝

Partnership

We pay 40% above Fair Trade minimum. Relationships last longer than transactions.

♻️

Responsibility

100% recyclable packaging. Zero single-use plastic. Carbon-offset shipping.
